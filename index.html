Instructions & Privacy Assurance

🔒 Your Privacy and Data Security Come First

  • This Resume Generator is 100% offline and privacy-focused.
  • No data is uploaded to any server, cloud storage, or third-party company.
  • We never sell, share, transmit, analyze, or collect your personal information.
  • All information you enter remains exclusively on your device and inside your browser session.
  • This application operates as a local HTML file and performs all processing directly within your browser.
  • Your data never leaves your computer unless you personally choose to save, print, or share your generated resume.
  • Profile photos, contact details, educational information, work experience, and all uploaded files are processed locally only.
  • No internet connection is required for creating, editing, or generating your resume.
  • No user accounts, registrations, tracking scripts, analytics tools, cookies, or background data collection mechanisms are used.

How to Use This Wizard

  • Navigate through the steps using the sidebar or "Next" buttons.
  • Fill in your personal and contact information accurately.
  • Use the "+ Add" buttons to include multiple experiences, education records, languages, or projects.
  • Select your preferred resume theme and layout style at the final step.
  • Click "Process & Render Document" to generate your resume preview.
  • Click "Print / Save PDF" on the preview screen to export.

Important Notes

  • Fields marked with (*) are mandatory. The wizard will prevent progression if they are empty.
  • The generated resume exists only within your current browser session. Closing the browser clears all data.
